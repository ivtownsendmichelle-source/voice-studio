Introduction

What is Voice Studio?

Voice Studio is a voice profile. A complete reference for how you write at every level of formality, built from your real writing.

Your writing changes depending on who you're talking to. A funder email reads differently than a board memo. A client thank-you reads differently than a vendor request. An Instagram caption reads differently than a LinkedIn post. Voice Studio captures that range. You define what each level of formality means for you. You describe how each format works.

When you finish, you'll have a complete voice profile on file. I review every submission personally and follow up within 72 hours.

This walks you through it in five steps. There are no wrong answers. The more honest your samples, the better it works.

Example. Facebook post: Short. One idea. No hashtags. Sometimes a question at the end.

Example. Client email: I lead with their name, acknowledge what they reached out about, then get to the point. I always close with a next step.
