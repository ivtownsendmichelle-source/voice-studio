Introduction

What is Voice Studio?

Voice Studio is a voice profile. A complete reference for how you write at every level of formality, built from your real writing.

Your writing changes depending on who you are talking to. A funder email reads differently than a board memo. A client thank-you reads differently than a vendor request. An Instagram caption reads differently than a LinkedIn post. Voice Studio captures that range. You define what each level of formality means for you. You describe how each format works.

When you finish, you will have a complete voice profile ready to share with AI writing assistants. You get two versions: one for direct paste into AI projects, and one XML file for system integrations.

This walks you through it in nine steps. There are no wrong answers. The more honest your samples, the better it works.

Voice Studio captures voice you already have, from writing you've already done. For Numbers where you want to develop a new voice (a platform you haven't written for yet), you can flag those and we will design that voice for you using the rest of your profile.
